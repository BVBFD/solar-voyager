## Asset Policy

- Store project texture files under `public/textures`.
- Record every imported image in this file before committing it.
- Prefer NASA/JPL public domain or clearly licensed sources.
- Solar System Scope textures must include CC BY 4.0 attribution.
- Do not download from sites with unclear ownership, scraped mirrors, wallpaper sites, or AI-upscaled reposts without a clear license.
- Do not bundle NASA logos, insignia, or agency branding.
